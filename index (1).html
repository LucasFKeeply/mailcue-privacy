Mailcue

Bulk schedule Gmail sends from a CSV — right from your browser.

Mailcue is a Chrome extension that lets you upload a CSV of recipients, personalise each email with merge tags, and schedule them to send through your own Gmail account. No CRM, no subscriptions to configure — just upload, preview, and schedule.

How it works

  • Upload a CSV with email addresses, subjects, and message bodies. Extra columns become personalisation tags like {{name}} or {{company}}.
  • Preview every email before sending. Mailcue auto-detects your columns and shows you exactly what each recipient will receive.
  • Schedule sends for a specific time or spread them across a window so they arrive naturally.
  • Emails send on time, even if you close Chrome. Server-side scheduling ensures delivery.

Privacy & security

Mailcue sends emails through your own Gmail account using the Gmail API. It only requests the permissions it needs: sending emails and reading your email address for account identification. Your email content is never stored longer than needed to complete the scheduled send. No tracking, no analytics on your email content, no third-party data sharing.
